Pork Banh Mi

A Vietnamese Classic

Banh Mi is a type of Vietnamese sandwich beloved all over the world

that consists usually of a type of protein, greens, picked veggies

all in a French baugette.

Ingredients

For the Pork:

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1/2 cup kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup lightly packed brown sugar
  • 4 cloves garlic, coarley chopped
  • 2 tablespoons whole black peppercorns
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 3 medium jalapenos, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup fish sauce
  • 3 pounds boneless pork shoulder, trimmed

For the Sandwich:

  • 1 French baguette, 22 to 24-inches long
  • 1/2 cup store-bought or homeade pate
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3/4 cup pickled carrot
  • 1 jalapeno, thinly sliced, seeded if desired
  • 3/4 cup thinly sliced seedless cucumber
  • 2/3 cup packed fresh cilantro

Steps for assembly

  1. Line both sides with mayonnaise
  2. Lie pork down first on baugette
  3. Add cucumbers, jalapaneos and pickled carrot
  4. garnish with cilantro
