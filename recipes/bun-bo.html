The Quintessential Vietnamese Noodle Soup

Bun bo Hue is a popilar Vietnamese rice noodle

dish with sliced beef, cha lua, and sometimes

pork knuckles

Ingredients

  • 2 lb beef shank
  • 2 lb oxtail
  • 2 lb pork hocks
  • 1 lb Hue style pork
  • 1 lb pork blood

Broth and Base & Seasoning

  • water
  • 24 oz chicken broth
  • 2 yellow onions, large
  • 3 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste
  • 3-4 tbsp
  • 2 tsp

Aromatics & Color

  • 3 tbsp anatto seeds
  • 3 tbsp neutral cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp shallot
  • 2 tbsp garlic

Herbs and Veg

  • mint
  • basil
  • bean sprouts
  • lime

Steps for Assembly

  1. Set noodles in bowl
  2. Pour Broth directly into bowl
  3. Add Meats
  4. Garnish with Herbs and Veg
